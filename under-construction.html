About This Experiment

I am building this website to expand my personal knowlege. I have no formal web development training and I expect that most, if not all, of my learning will be self-taught. Since I am trying to learn, I expect this website to have various random elements that may not necessarily fit together logically, but will nonetheless expand my knowlege.

My plan is to continue to make changes and add to the website as I go along. I started letting everyone have access as soon as I had a basic home page that mostly worked even though I knew the website was minimally complete and had little, if anything, of interest. Hopefully, at some point I will be able to figure out how to receive comments so that I can receive feedback.

Importantly, I am trying to make sure that I only use material that is freely available and give necessary attribution. However, if you believe I have inappropriately used your material, please let me know so that I can promptly remove it or make appropriate changes (note: I realize that you can't notify me at this point, but hopefully I'll figure it out soon).

Thank you for visiting.
