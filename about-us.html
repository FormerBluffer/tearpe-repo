About Me

I grew up in Illinois, but currently live in Montana. I have also lived in Indiana and California. Each of these places has provided experiences that are quite different. As a result, I have come to appreciate that just about everywhere has great things to offer if you are willing to try new things.

I also enjoy traveling. I have been to over 25 countries so far. Once again, even though I have found many of the places I have visited to be quite different, I have found that people are mostly friendly and helpful no matter where you are. The more I travel, the more I appreciate the wide range of great experiences you can have if you are willing to explore a little.
